Description: Syntax Tree (AST). STG nodes are created as appropriate statements are encountered in the AST. Any contiguous sequence of other statements representing computation that are executed by the same set of processors are grouped into a single computational task (contiguous implies that they are not interrupted by any of the above statements or by communication). 4 Identifying statements that are computed by the same set of processors is a critical aspect of the above step.
