Subject: Onno Boxma,Ger Koole,Zhen Liu Queueing-Theoretic Solution Methods for Models of Parallel and Distributed Systems

Description: This paper aims to give an overview of solution methods for the performance analysis of parallel and distributed systems. After a brief review of some important general solution methods, we discuss key models of parallel and distributed systems, and optimization issues, from the viewpoint of solution methodology.
