Title: Relationships between Models of Concurrency

Author:

Author:

Author:

Subject: Mogens Nielsen,Vladimiro Sassone,Glynn Winskel Relationships between Models of Concurrency

Description: . Models for concurrency can be classified with respect to three relevant parameters: behaviour/system, interleaving/noninterleaving, linear/branching time. When modelling a process, a choice concerning such parameters corresponds to choosing the level of abstraction of the resulting semantics. The classifications are formalized through the medium of category theory. Keywords. Semantics, Concurrency, Models for Concurrency, Categories.
