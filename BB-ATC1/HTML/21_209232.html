Description: With the recent expansion of the Internet, the interest towards electronic sales has quickly grown and many tools have been built to help vendors to set up their Web stores. These tools offer all the facilities for building the store databases and managing the order processing and secure payment transactions, but they typically do not focus on issues like the personalization of the interaction with the customers. However, Web surfers are generally heterogeneous and have different needs and preferences; moreover, the trend of marketing strategies is to pay more and more attention to the specific buyers. So, the importance of personalizing the interaction with the user and the product presentation is increasing. In this paper, we describe the architecture of a configurable virtual Web store supporting personalized hypertextual interactions with users.
