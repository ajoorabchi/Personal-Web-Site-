Title: A CBR Integration From Inception to Productization

Author:

Subject: Lisa Purvis A CBR Integration From Inception to Productization

Description: Our case-based reasoning (CBR) integration with the constraint satisfaction problem (CSP) formalism has undergone several transformations on its journey from initial research idea to product-intent design. Both unexpected research results as well as interesting insights into the real-world applicability of the integrated methodology emerged as the integration was explored from alternative viewpoints. In this paper, the alternative viewpoints and the results that were enabled by these viewpoints are described. Introduction Although Case-Based Reasoning is a natural formulation for many problems, there are elements of the paradigm that prevent the methodology from being more widely applied.
