Title: A Facility For Experimenting Distributed Software in the Internet

Author:

Author:

Subject: Yongguang Zhang,Bharat Bhargava A Facility For Experimenting Distributed Software in the Internet

Description: To test distributed software in the Internet we need many experimental sites in different physical locations. To ease the experimental setup, we have developed a facility for a study of distributed transaction processing in the Internet. The facility emulates remote sites by local machines, and emulates a true Internet communication environment in a local area network, by routing the inter-site communication through actual Internet hosts. This provides a mechanism to conduct experiments without any designated experimental sites except for the local ones, yet the results are as genuine as in the real experiments. It also supports a trace-driven emulation method that measures the inter-site communication of the Internet and uses the measurement data to generate empirical values for delay and message loss between two local sites.
