Title: Trilinearity of Three Perspective Views and its Associated Tensor

Author:

Author:

Subject: Amnon Shashua,Michael Werman Trilinearity of Three Perspective Views and its Associated Tensor

Description: It has been established that certain trilinear froms of three perspective views give rise to a tensor of 27 intrinsic coefficients [11]. We show in this paper that a permutation of the the trilinear coefficients produces three homography matrices (projective transformations of planes) of three distinct intrinsic planes, respectively. This, in turn, yields the result that 3D invariants are recovered directly --- simply by appropriate arrangement of the tensor's coefficients. On a secondary level, we show new relations between fundamental matrix, epipoles, Euclidean structure and the trilinear tensor.
