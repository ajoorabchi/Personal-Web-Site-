Author:

Author:

Subject: Richard Kennaway,Jan Willem Klop Comparing Curried and Uncurried Rewriting

Description: this paper we show that strong normalization (SN), weak normalization (WN), the weak Church-Rosser property (WCR), the unique normal form property (UN), completeness, and semi-completeness are preserved by currying. For left-linear term rewrite systems we show that currying also preserves the normal form property (NF) and the UN
