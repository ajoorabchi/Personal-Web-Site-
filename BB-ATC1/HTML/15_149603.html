Title: Characteristics of Multiplexed Variable Bit Rate Video Sources

Author:

Author:

Subject: Guohu Huang,Y Goebel Characteristics of Multiplexed Variable Bit Rate Video Sources

Description: Multimedia applications will play a crucial role in future highspeed networks. Despite improvements in network switching and transmission capacity, the anticipated volume of multimedia traffic will remain a challenge, and any results which characterize multimedia data streams will be useful in understanding how to improve their distribution over digital networks. Clarification of the statistical characteristics of multimedia traffic, especially video traffic, is therefore of great significance. Here we present a statistical analysis of one hour long sample of the aggregated traffic of three synthetic MPEG video sources. The sample was obtained from a simple network model running on the ATM-TN simulator, which was developed by the Western University Research Consortium on High Performance Computing and Networking (WurcNet) TeleSim Project.
