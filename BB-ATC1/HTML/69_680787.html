Description: Degrees will be listed as: B.Sc. cum laude, BRIGHAM YOUNG UNIVERSITY M.Sc., UNIVERSITY OF MASSACHUSETTS AMHERST Ph.D., UNIVERSITY OF MASSACHUSETTS AMHERST Directed by: Professor W. Bruce Croft The content-based retrieval of Western music has received increasing attention in recent years. While much of this research deals with monophonic music, polyphonic music is far more common and more interesting, encompassing a wide selection of classical to popular music. Polyphony is also far more complex, with multiple overlapping notes per time step, in comparison with monophonic music's one-dimensional sequence of notes. Many of the techniques developed for monophonic music retrieval either break down or are simply not applicable to polyphony.
