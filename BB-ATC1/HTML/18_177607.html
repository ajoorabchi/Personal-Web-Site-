Title: Interactive Visualization of Implicit Surfaces with Singularities

Author:

Author:

Author:

Subject: Angela Rosch,Matthias Ruhl,Dietmar Saupe Interactive Visualization of Implicit Surfaces with Singularities

Description: This paper presents work on two methods for interactive visualization of implicit surfaces: physicallybased sampling using particle systems and polygonization followed by physically-based mesh improvement which explicitly makes use of the surface-defining equation. While most previous work applied to bounded manifolds without singularities and without boundary (topological spheres) we broaden the scope of the methods to include surfaces with such features, in particular cusp points and surface selfintersections. These aspects are not (yet) essential for computer graphics modelling with implicit surfaces but they naturally occur in simulations of interest in mathematical visualization. In this paper we use the Kummer family of algebraic surfaces as an example. 1 Introduction Our work is motivated by efforts in the mathematical community for the visualization of implicit algebraic surfaces.
