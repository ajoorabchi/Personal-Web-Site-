Coupled with recent and considerable gains in processor performance and network capabilities, parallel environments consisting of clustered workstations remain viable platforms for high-performance computation. These recent gains in hardware capabilities have evolved hand-in-hand with significant developments in software tools, programming languages and programming methodologies --- most notably, the languages and paradigms associated with Internet programming.
