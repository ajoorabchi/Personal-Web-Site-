Subject: Jonathan Bowen,C. A. R. Hoare,Hans Langmaack,Anders P. Ravn A ProCoS II Project Final Report: ESPRIT Basic Research project 7071

Description: An overview of the research and associated activities of the European collaborative ESPRIT Basic Research ProCoS II project (no. 7071) on "Provably Correct Systems" which ran from 1992 to 1995 is presented. This was a follow-on project to ProCoS (no. 3104) and ran in parallel with the ProCoS-WG Working Group (no. 8694), all previously announced in the Bulletin of the EATCS [3, 17, 18]. A comprehensive bibliography of publications with selected project documents is included for those wishing to study the results of the project in greater depth.
