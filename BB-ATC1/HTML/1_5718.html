Title: Efficient Call Handling Procedures in Cellular Mobile Networks

Author:

Author:

Subject: Michel Mandjes,Kurt Tutschku Efficient Call Handling Procedures in Cellular Mobile Networks

Description: This paper discusses two advanced schemes, namely guard channels and the retry of blocked handovers. Furthermore, a more detailed customer model is presented in this study in order to obtain a more acccurate cell configuration: instead of the usual Poisson input, we allow the blocked fresh calls to be repeated. We show how this model can mathematically be analyzed and evaluate the efficiency of the call handling mechanisms.
