Title: Wavelets on Irregular Point Sets

Subject: Wavelets on Irregular Point Sets

Description: this article we review techniques for building and analyzing wavelets on irregular point sets in one and two dimensions. We discuss current results both on the practical and theoretical side. In particular we focus on subdivision schemes and commutation rules. Several examples are included.
