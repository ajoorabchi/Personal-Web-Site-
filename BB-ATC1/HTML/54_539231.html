Title: Content-based Video Communication: Methodology and Applications

Author:

Subject: Paul Bocheck Content-based Video Communication: Methodology and Applications

Description: Most traditional video communication systems consider videos as large files or long bit streams, ignoring the underlying visual content. We introduce a new content-aware framework that explores the strong correlation between video content, resource (bit rate), and utility (quality). Content-based video communication is a promising approach that seamlessly integrates video compression and networking technologies aimed at overcoming current limitations of multimedia communication technology.
