Subject: Barbara Dinter,Carsten Sapia,Markus Blaschka,Gabriele Hfling OLAP Market and Research: Initiating the Cooperation

Description: The main idea of a data warehouse is to provide decision makers with integrated information that is organized according to their requirements. A considerable share of the rapidly evolving data warehouse market is held by products for building OLAP systems as these are the predominant frontend tools in decision making environments. These products include mainly OLAP databases but also design and maintenance tools. Due to permanently increasing business needs, technical development in the OLAP area is strongly market driven. Consequently existing tools constitute the state of the art. On the other hand, database researchers apply former results to the innovative field of data warehousing and OLAP. However, OLAP related research issues have not attracted as much attention as the success of OLAP products in the market would imply.
