There are many types of biological societies formed by animals, but societies formed by machines or robots are still rare. This paper introduces the concept of an application oriented robot society formed by autonomous underwater robots. This bacterium robot society is a generic concept with many potential applications. As an example of such application we present a scenario, where robot society serves as a mobile distributed sensor instrument inside a process plant. 1.
